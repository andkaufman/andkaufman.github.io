LEGAL SERVICES: Business, Real Estate and Estate Planning

Why do you need a lawyer?

As a small business owner, there are constantly legal matters and issues that present themselves throughout the year. Whether serious or minor, Andrew Kaufman has always been the one we have turned to for advice on how to properly address the situation.

What should you look for in a law firm?

When looking for an attorney, it’s important to find someone who will take the time to fully explain the legal situation and all of the possible ways to handle it. The law can be complicated and difficult to understand, but Andrew always explains everything in a way that’s easy to understand.

Would you recommend Andrew Kaufman LLC?

For us, Andrew's honesty and kindness is what makes him a great attorney. He’s a great family man, and we can always approach him, and he’s there to greet us with a smile. He knows his job extremely well and takes great care in all of his work.
