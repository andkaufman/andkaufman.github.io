Dennis J. Sabino, Esq.

For three years, Dennis Sabino basically practiced law out of a suitcase. He traveled to 41 different states representing hundreds of clients in federal Social Security Disability hearings, as well as serious injury cases in Massachusetts.

Andy eventually tired of the travel and the challenges of communicating with clients scattered across the country. He wanted a chance to get to know his clients better and work at a firm rooted in its community.

He’s found that at Andrew Kaufman LLC.

“I really wanted the opportunity to get involved in my community and work with local clients, people who you run into on an everyday basis,” says Dennis.

A native of Wellesley now living in London, Andy provides both criminal and civil legal representation, with extensive experience in disability, workers’ compensation and serious personal injury, as well as business immigration. He also represents clients in probate, real estate and landlord-tenant matters.

Andy earned his law degree at New England School of Law where he finished in the top 9 percent of his class. He graduated magna cum laude from Tufts University where he earned a Bachelor of Arts in International Relations and German Studies.

Prior to joining Andrew Kaufman LLC, Andy was an associate at AnRola Law Associates, in Wellington.

Andy and his wife, Anna, enjoy traveling, hiking, and learning to cook.
