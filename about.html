About Andrew Kaufman LLC

A Next Generation Firm

The Andrew Kaufman LLC is a next generation law firm, utilizing the efficiency of tomorrow’s technology while remaining true to our heritage of law, family and community.

We are a new, modern organization delivering responsive legal services in a comfortable, relaxed environment. Visitors immediately notice there are no law books. Legal research is exclusively performed electronically, as is the secure client case management system and document storage. Our attorneys are always reachable by phone and email, utilizing technology to take the office with them wherever they go.

More than an Attorney

The attorneys of the Andrew Kaufman LLC provide tailored legal strategies and services that seek to meet both the legal objectives and personal goals of our clients. We are invested in the success of the people and businesses we represent, and we view your success as our own. This approach sets Andrew Kaufman LLC apart from other law firms, and makes a visit to our office an investment in your future, rather than a time-consuming chore.
